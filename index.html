Red-Flag Triage Escalation

When minutes matter, our triage system enters "High Alert" mode. If critical symptoms are detected (such as signs of anaphylaxis, stroke, or pulmonary embolism), the system bypasses standard questionnaires to provide immediate emergency instructions.

Emergency Logic Matrix:

If [Chest Pain] + [Shortness of Breath] = Alert Level 1 (Immediate EMS)

If [High Fever] + [Stiff Neck] = Alert Level 2 (Urgent Care)

Doctor-Ready Clinical Summaries

Communication is the bridge to healing. SymptoScore generates a structured SBAR (Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation) report that you can present to your physician. This reduces "history-taking" time and allows for faster treatment.

  • Streamlined Consultations: Hand over a professional report instead of trying to remember every detail.
  • Medical Terminology: Translates your symptoms into clinical language for faster physician review.
  • Exportable Formats: Download as a secure PDF or send directly to your EHR-integrated portal.
